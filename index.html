Why should you care?

According to Cornell University, about 40 percent of deaths worldwide are caused by water, air and soil pollution. Besides malnutrition, water pollution is also a direct cause of 6 million children's deaths each year.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate the youth with a problem in our world that is not really addressed about. We want to spread the message how water all around the world is being polluted and there aren't many people taking action. With our project, we hope to share a message worldwide that anyone can help end water pollution whether it's by volunteering with others to clean waterways. Even if you're busy and can't physically volunteer, you can raise awareness to a specific ocean or river that is polluted and hasn't been cared for. We want our users to be informed about water pollution but also be encourage to help the water.

About Water Pollution:

Water pollution is the contamination of water systems of harmful substances and pollutants. These substances prove harmful to animals and people. Water pollution is caused by littering, industrial waste, oil spills and global warming.
